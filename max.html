Max's Highlights!

Max has had an incredible year! He turned 4 in April. He already knows he wants to have his 5th birthday party at a place with Laser Tag. He has really developed into his own small human. Max loves to roughhouse and play outside. Everything Max does is big. He has big feelings, he loves fiercely, gives great big hugs, and he has a big voice. Like a huge voice. Like he can wake up the whole neighborhood. Did I mention he can get loud?

Max started at a new school in August. He loves learning with his new friends at Children's Garden Montessori. Max comes home singing songs, counting to 100, and telling stories about some of his favorite friends in school. We are so happy he is in a place that allows him to be himself. He's sharp as a tack and is one of the most perceptive humans you will ever meet.

Max is the master of asking questions. He asks them rapid fire and often doesn't even allow room to answer before asking the next one. He is curious about everything and always asks, "Why?" He could make a fantastic litigator or scientist. He wins every argument and doesn't stop until he is satisfied with an answer.

Max will be heading to Kindergarten in the fall. He is really excited to go to the same school as his brother. We are really excited for him to keep growing and make his mark on the world. We're also pretty excited for what it will mean for our bank account!

We are so excited to see Max grow this year. We can't wait to see who he will be in the coming year!
