Zach's Highlights!

2023 has been a fun year for Zach. It has been a year of growth and learning to try to take more time for himself

Zach continues to teach at Ignite Online Academy in Durham Public Schools. He loves the flexibility that teaching at an online school provides. It has given him a chance to sharpen more of his instructional skills and made him a more rounded educator. He has taken on more of a leadership role within his school and is working with staff to develop effective online courses and instruction.

Zach and his friend AJ have continued to grow their record business, Three Count Records. They have installed a full-time booth at Gizmo Brew Works in Durham and will be setting up a second booth at the new Dissolvr taproom. Zach DJs on Thursday nights at Gizmo along with AJ. TCR continues to do pop-up record sales around the triangle at places like Beer Study and the Punk Rock Flea Market. The TCR Podcast is on an extended break as we look to reformat.

Overall, 2023 was a great year for Zach. It was filled with time with his family, his second visit to the West Coast, starting new projects, and trying to be the best partner he can be.

Bring on 2024!
